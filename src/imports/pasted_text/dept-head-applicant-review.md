Update the **Department Head Applicant Review page** so that its layout, structure, and components match the **Applicant Management page used in the HR Officer interface**.

Do not change the HRIS design system, colors, typography, card styles, spacing, or layout components. Maintain the same UI style used in the HR Officer pages.

---

NAVIGATION STRUCTURE

Under the **Recruitment** or **Applicant Review** section in the Department Head sidebar, open the **Applicant Review page**.

This page should visually match the **Applicant Management page in the HR Officer view**.

---

PAGE HEADER

Left side:

Applicant List

Right side button:

Upload New Application

Description under the title:

View and manage job applicants with automated evaluation scores.

---

TOP RANKED APPLICANTS SECTION

Display a section called:

Top Ranked Applicants

Show the **Top 1 – Top 5 applicants** based on the system’s automated evaluation score.

Each ranking card should display:

Rank Number
Applicant Name
Position Applied
Total Score

This section must match the ranking layout used in the HR Officer interface.

---

APPLICANTS SECTION

Below the ranking section create a **two-column layout**.

LEFT COLUMN — APPLICANT LIST

Display applicants in a list or table.

Fields include:

Applicant Number (#)
Applicant Name
Position Applied
Application Date
Status

Include search and filters.

Applicant numbers must match the ranking order.

---

RIGHT COLUMN — EVALUATION PANEL

When an applicant is selected, display the evaluation panel.

Show the automated evaluation breakdown:

Education Score
Eligibility Score
Training Score
Experience Score
Total Score

Display evaluation status such as:

Qualified
Not Qualified
Under Review

The evaluation panel must match the design used in the HR Officer interface.

---

HIRING RECOMMENDATION

Department Heads cannot finalize hiring decisions.

Instead, include buttons:

Recommend for Hiring
Do Not Recommend

These recommendations will be forwarded to the **HR Officer**, who will make the final hiring decision.

Status examples:

Recommended
Not Recommended
Pending HR Decision

---

DATA SYNCHRONIZATION

Ensure that the following remain synchronized across the page:

Applicant Number
Ranking Position
Evaluation Scores
Application Status

The Top Ranked Applicants section, Applicant List, and Evaluation Panel must all reflect the same data.

---

GOAL

Make the **Department Head Applicant Review interface visually identical to the HR Officer Applicant Management interface**, while allowing Department Heads to **review applicants and submit recommendations instead of final hiring decisions**.
