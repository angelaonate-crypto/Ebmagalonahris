Update all **My Profile pages across the HRIS** so that they match the **Department Head My Profile layout**, which should serve as the **reference design**.

This applies to:

• Employee My Profile
• HR Staff My Profile
• Department Head My Profile
• HR Officer My Profile (if available)

Do not change the HRIS design system, colors, typography, header layout, sidebar structure, card components, spacing, or icons.

Only standardize the **content structure and completeness of the PDS profile sections**.

---

REFERENCE PROFILE

Use the **Department Head My Profile page** as the design reference.

All other My Profile pages must match:

• Layout structure
• Section order
• Card style
• Table layout
• Field grouping

Profile Photo

PDS SECTION STRUCTURE

Ensure the profile contains **all sections of the Personal Data Sheet (CS Form 212)**.

Sections must appear in this order.

---

SECTION I — PERSONAL INFORMATION

Fields include:

Surname
First Name
Middle Name
Name Extension

Date of Birth
Place of Birth
Sex at Birth
Civil Status
Citizenship

Physical Information:

Height
Weight
Blood Type

Government IDs:

UMID ID No.
PAG-IBIG ID No.
PhilHealth No.
PhilSys No.
TIN No.
Agency Employee No.

Addresses:

Residential Address
Permanent Address

Contact Information:

Telephone Number
Mobile Number
Email Address

---

SECTION II — FAMILY BACKGROUND

Fields include:

Spouse Name
Spouse Occupation
Employer / Business Name
Business Address
Telephone Number

Parents:

Father's Name
Mother's Maiden Name

Children Table:

Child Name
Date of Birth

---

SECTION III — EDUCATIONAL BACKGROUND

Display as table:

Level
Name of School
Degree / Course
Period of Attendance (From – To)
Highest Level / Units Earned
Year Graduated
Scholarships / Academic Honors

Levels:

Elementary
Secondary
Vocational / Trade Course
College
Graduate Studies

---

SECTION IV — CIVIL SERVICE ELIGIBILITY

Display table:

Eligibility Type
Rating
Date of Examination
Place of Examination
License Number
Validity Date

---

SECTION V — WORK EXPERIENCE

Display work history table.

Columns:

Inclusive Dates (From – To)
Position Title
Department / Agency / Office / Company
Status of Appointment
Government Service (Yes / No)

Include expandable details:

Accomplishments
Summary of Duties

Work experience must be ordered **from most recent to oldest**.

---

SECTION VI — VOLUNTARY WORK

Table fields:

Organization Name
Inclusive Dates

Position / Nature of Work

---

SECTION VII — LEARNING AND DEVELOPMENT

Table fields:

Training Title

Type of Training
Conducted / Sponsored By

---

SECTION VIII — OTHER INFORMATION

Fields include:

Special Skills and Hobbies
Non-Academic Distinctions / Recognition
Membership in Associations / Organizations

---

SECTION IX — REFERENCES

Table fields:

Reference Name
Office / Address
Contact Number / Email

---

DECLARATION / NOTE SECTION

Ensure the profile page includes the **final declaration section from the PDS**, which appears near the end of the form.

Include fields for:

Government Issued ID
ID Number
Date / Place of Issuance
Digital Signature Field
Date Accomplished

Include the **PDS declaration note stating that the information provided is true and correct**.

---

GOAL

Ensure that **all My Profile pages across the HRIS are complete and consistent**, using the **Department Head My Profile page as the reference**, and displaying the full **Personal Data Sheet (CS Form 212) content up to the declaration/note section**.
